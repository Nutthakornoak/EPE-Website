Company Profile

History :

EPE Packaging (Thailand) Co., Ltd. was established in 1994 as a member of EPE International Group, a leading total industrial packaging and foamed plastic solutions provider with over 70 offices in 10 countries worldwide. With over four decades of experience in the packaging and cushioning industry, EPE Packaging (Thailand) Co., Ltd. has been serving and satisfying customers with innovative packaging solutions.

Since its establishment, EPE Packaging (Thailand) Co., Ltd. has been committed to providing high-quality packaging products and services to its customers. The company has a strong focus on customer satisfaction and aims to be a one-stop service provider for multi-national customers by managing international supply programs and supporting supply chains locally.

Over the years, EPE Packaging (Thailand) Co., Ltd. has expanded its operations and capabilities to meet the evolving needs of its customers. The company has invested in in-house design, performance testing, manufacturing, warehousing, and business alliances to ensure efficient and effective service delivery.

Today, EPE Packaging (Thailand) Co., Ltd. continues to uphold its vision of being Thailand's leading total industrial packaging and foamed plastic solutions provider in a collaborative global network. The company's mission is to achieve customer-centric sustainable growth by dedicating resources and enabling its people to commit to excellence in the packaging industry.
