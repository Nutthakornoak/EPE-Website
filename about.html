About

Overview :

A part of over four decades of EPE Group's experience (Est.1973 in Japan) in packaging & cushioning industry, EPE Packaging (Thailand) Co.Ltd. was established in Thailand more than twenty years (Est.1994) for serving and satisfying customers by our "Leading Total Industrial Packaging and Foamed Plastic Solutions Provider"

As a member of EPE International group, we are keen to manage international supply program and serve as one-stop-service for our multi-national customers In addition, our in-house design, performance testing, manufacturing, warehousing and business alliance are in-line to manage and support supply chain of our customers, locally

Vision :

Thailand's Leading Total Industrial Packaging and Foamed Plastic Solutions Provider in a Collaborative Global Networks

Mission :

To Be Customer-Centric Sustainable Growth and a Leading Industrial Packaging and Foamed Plastic Solutions Provider in Thailand, Our Enabled People Commits and Efficiently Dedicates our Resources
